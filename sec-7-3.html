    \(\boldsymbol{f(t)}\) \(\mathcal{L}\boldsymbol{\{f(t)\}=F(s)}\)
    1.    \(1\) \(\dfrac{1}{s},\) \(s>0\)
    2. \(t\) \(\dfrac{1}{s^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    3. \(t^{n},\; n\mbox{ being a natural number}\) \(\dfrac{n!}{s^{\,n+1}},\) \(s>0\)
    4. \(t^{-1/2}\) \(\dfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{\sqrt{s}},\) \(s>0\)
    5. \(t^{1/2}\) \(\dfrac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2\,s^{3/2}},\) \(s>0\)
    6. \(t^{\alpha},\ \alpha >-1\) \(\dfrac{\Gamma(\alpha+1)}{s^{\alpha+1}},\) \(s>0\)
    7. \(\sin(kt)\) \(\dfrac{k}{s^{2}+k^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    8. \(\cos(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s}{s^{2}+k^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    9. \(\sin^{2}(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2k^{2}}{s(s^{2}+4k^{2})},\) \(s>0\)
    10. \(\cos^{2}(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s^{2}+2k^{2}}{s(s^{2}+4k^{2})},\) \(s>0\)
    11. \(e^{at}\) \(\dfrac{1}{s-a},\) \(s>a\)
    12. \(\sinh(k t)\) \(\dfrac{k}{s^{2}-k^{2}},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    13. \(\cosh(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s}{s^{2}-k^{2}},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    14. \(\sinh^{2}(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2k^{2}}{s(s^{2}-4k^{2})},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    15. \(\cosh^{2}(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s^{2}-2k^{2}}{s(s^{2}-4k^{2})},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    16. \(t e^{at}\) \(\dfrac{1}{(s-a)^{2}},\) \(s>a\)
    17. \(t^{n}e^{at},\; n\mbox{ being a natural number}\) \(\dfrac{n!}{(s-a)^{n+1}},\) \(s>a\)
    18. \(e^{at}\sin(kt)\) \(\dfrac{k}{(s-a)^{2}+k^{2}},\) \(s>a\)

    20. \(e^{at}\sinh(kt)\) \(\dfrac{k}{(s-a)^{2}-k^{2}},\) \(s>a+\lvert k\rvert\)
    21. \(e^{at}\cosh(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s-a}{(s-a)^{2}-k^{2}},\) \(s>a+\lvert k\rvert\)
    22. \(t\sin(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2ks}{(s^{2}+k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    23. \(t\cos(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s^{2}-k^{2}}{(s^{2}+k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    24. \(\sin(kt)+kt\cos(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2k s^{2}}{(s^{2}+k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    25. \(\sin(kt)-kt\cos(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2k^{3}}{(s^{2}+k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>0\)
    26. \(t\sinh(kt)\) \(\dfrac{2ks}{(s^{2}-k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    27. \(t\cosh(kt)\) \(\dfrac{s^{2}+k^{2}}{(s^{2}-k^{2})^{2}},\) \(s>\lvert k\rvert\)
    28. \(\dfrac{e^{at}-e^{bt}}{a-b}\) \(\dfrac{1}{(s-a)(s-b)},\) \(s>\max\{a, b\}\)
    29. \(\dfrac{ae^{at}-be^{bt}}{a-b}\) \(\dfrac{s}{(s-a)(s-b)},\) \(s>\max\{a, b\}\)
    30. \(1-\cos(kt)\) \(\dfrac{k^{2}}{s(s^{2}+k^{2})},\) \(s>0\)
    31. \(kt-\sin(kt)\) \(\dfrac{k^{3}}{s^{2}(s^{2}+k^{2})},\) \(s>0\)
    32. \(\dfrac{a\sin(bt)-b\sin(at)}{ab(a^{2}-b^{2})}\) \(\dfrac{1}{(s^{2}+a^{2})(s^{2}+b^{2})},\) \(s>0\)

    Subsection 6.3.2 Derivatives

    We use notations \(\dfrac{d}{dx}f(x)\) and \(f'(x)\) to synonymously denote the derivative of \(f(x)\) with respect to \(x.\)
    🔗
    Differentiation Rules
    🔗
    Constant: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}c = 0\)
    Constant Multiple: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\big(cf(x)\big) = c f'(x)\)
    Sum: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\!\big(f(x)\pm g(x)\big) = f'(x)\pm g'(x)\)
    Product: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\big(f(x)g(x)\big) = f'(x)g(x) + g'(x)f(x)\)
    Quotient: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right) = \frac{g(x)f'(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{[g(x)]^{2}}\)
    Chain: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}f(g(x)) = f'(g(x))\,g'(x)\)
    Power: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}x^{n}= n x^{n-1}\)
    Power: \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}[g(x)]^{n}= n[g(x)]^{n-1}g'(x)\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\sin x = \cos x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\cos x = -\sin x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\tan x = \sec^{2}x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\cot x = -\csc^{2}x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\sec x = \sec x\tan x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\csc x = -\csc x\cot x\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\sin^{-1}x = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\cos^{-1}x = -\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^{2}}}\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\tan^{-1}x = \frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\cot^{-1}x = -\frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\sec^{-1}x = \frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^{2}-1}}\)
    \(\ds \frac{d}{dx}\csc^{-1}x = -\frac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^{2}-1}}\)
