Theorem 5.1.15. Invertibility of the Laplace transform.

Let \(f\) and \(g\) be two piecewise continuous functions defined on \([0, \infty)\) such that their Laplace transforms exist for \(s > s_0\) for some real number \(s_0.\) If
\begin{equation*} \mathcal {L}\{f(t)\} = \mathcal {L}\{g(t)\}, \end{equation*}
then \(f(t) = g(t)\) for all \(t\ge 0\) at which both \(f\) and \(g\) are continuous.
