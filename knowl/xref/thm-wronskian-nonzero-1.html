Theorem 3.1.13. (Wronskian Test for Linear Independence).

Let \(y_1, \dots, y_k\) be \(k-1\) times differentiable functions on an interval \(I\) with
\begin{equation*} W(y_1, \dots, y_k)(x)\ne 0 \mbox{ on } I. \end{equation*}
Then \(y_1, \dots, y_k\) are linearly independent on \(I.\)
