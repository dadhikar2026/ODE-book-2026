Example 3.1.12.

Compute the Wronskian of \(y_1=e^x\) and \(y_2=xe^{-x}\) on \((-\infty, \infty).\)
🔗
Solution.
The Wronskian \(W(y_1,y_2)(x)\) of \(y_1=e^x\) and \(y_2=xe^{-x}\) is
\begin{equation*} W(e^x,xe^{-x}) = \begin{vmatrix} y_1(x) & y_2(x) \\ y_1'(x) & y_2'(x) \\ \end{vmatrix} = \begin{vmatrix} e^x & xe^{-x} \\ e^x & e^{-x}-xe^{-x} \\ \end{vmatrix} = 1-2x \end{equation*}
on \((-\infty, \infty).\)
